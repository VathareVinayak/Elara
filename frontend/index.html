How Elara Works

Elara combines cutting-edge AI with sophisticated document processing to deliver accurate, context-aware responses.

1

Document Upload & Processing

Upload your PDF documents which are automatically chunked and processed for optimal retrieval.

2

Vector Embedding Generation

Each document chunk is converted into vector embeddings using AI models and stored in the vector database.

3

Query Processing

When you ask a question, Elara normalizes your query and converts it to vector embeddings for similarity search.

4

Semantic Search

Elara performs k-NN search with cosine similarity to find the most relevant document chunks for your query.

5

Context Augmentation

Relevant document chunks are formatted into a system prompt with proper citations and context.

6

AI Response Generation

The AI generates accurate answers based on the augmented context, with sources properly cited.
